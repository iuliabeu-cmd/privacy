Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Our application may collect basic information such as your username, profile details, or content you choose to share through the Instagram API. We do not store or share sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used only for the functionality of the app (e.g., posting content on your behalf). We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Retention

We do not store your Instagram data on our servers. Any temporary data is deleted after the publishing process is completed.

Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like your data to be deleted, you can request removal at any time using the following steps:

1. Manual Deletion Request

Send us an email at lolcoachingromania@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request”. We will process your request within 7 days.

2. Account Disconnection

You can revoke the app’s access to your Instagram account at any time by visiting your Facebook/Instagram account settings → Apps and Websites → Active Apps → Remove Access.

3. Automatic Data Deletion

All temporary data used by the application (such as post metadata) is automatically deleted from our systems after the task is complete.
